About Bangladesh Teleradiology Association

The Bangladesh Teleradiology Association (BTA) is a professional body dedicated to advancing and regulating teleradiology services in Bangladesh.
Our mission is to ensure accurate and ethical radiology reporting, improve patient care, and create a trusted network of qualified professionals who uphold the highest standards in medical imaging.

Regulation & Membership Policy

Membership in Bangladesh Teleradiology Association (BTA) is mandatory for all individuals and organizations that provide radiological reporting services through teleradiology in Bangladesh.

  • Only BTA-approved members are authorized to operate teleradiology reporting services, as endorsed by the Bangladesh Radiology Society and aligned with government policy.
  • Companies or hospitals that engage in reporting services without BTA membership will face strict action in collaboration with the Bangladesh Radiology Society.
  • Through membership, professionals commit to maintaining accuracy, ethical practice, and accountability in every report, ensuring patient safety and quality healthcare delivery.

By enforcing these policies, BTA safeguards the integrity of radiology services and protects patients from unethical and substandard practices.
