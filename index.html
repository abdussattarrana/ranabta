About Bangladesh Teleradiology Association

The Bangladesh Teleradiology Association (BTA) is a professional organization formed to unite all teleradiology service providers across Bangladesh. Our goal is to enhance the quality of remote radiology services and foster collaboration among healthcare professionals and institutions. Through advocacy, education, and professional development, we aim to establish high standards in diagnostic imaging.

Medical Accuracy

Ensuring high-quality reporting through verified professionals.

Nationwide Collaboration

Bringing together experts and centers across the country.

Bangladesh Teleradiology Association Committee

The BTA Committee consists of esteemed professionals dedicated to ensuring the effective functioning of the association and the advancement of teleradiology in Bangladesh. The committee oversees strategic decisions, operational management, and advocacy for the industry.

President

Leads the association and oversees the strategic direction, representing BTA at national and international forums.

Vice President

Assists the President in all responsibilities and steps in when the President is unavailable, ensuring smooth operational flow.

General Secretary

Manages all communication and documentation for the association, coordinating with members and stakeholders.

Press Secretary

Responsible for managing the media relations and public image of the association. The Press Secretary coordinates communication between the BTA and the media, issues press releases, and ensures that key information is disseminated to the public and stakeholders.

Treasurer

Responsible for managing the financial health of the association, including budgeting and fundraising efforts.

Medical Advisor

Oversees clinical quality and advises on best practices in radiology, ensuring that teleradiology services meet medical and ethical standards.

IT & Technology Officer

Manages the technological infrastructure of the association, ensuring the security, functionality, and efficiency of all systems related to teleradiology services.

Legal Advisor

Provides legal guidance to the association and ensures compliance with regulatory and legal requirements related to teleradiology and telemedicine.
