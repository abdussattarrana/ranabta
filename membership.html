Membership Details

Become a certified member of Bangladesh Teleradiology Association (BTA) to ensure ethical, accurate and accountable radiology reporting services.

Criteria to be a Member

  • Registered radiology companies and diagnostic centers offering teleradiology services in Bangladesh.
  • Directors, radiologists or professionals actively engaged in teleradiology services.
  • Commitment to BTA rules, ethical guidelines, and service standards.

Documents Needed for Membership

  • Completed BTA Membership Application Form.
  • Copy of trade license (for companies) or BMDC registration (for individuals).
  • Valid National ID / Passport copy.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.

Membership Fee

Company Membership: Tk. 25,000 (one-time registration + yearly renewal).
Individual / Director Membership: Tk. 5,000 (one-time registration + yearly renewal).

Benefits of Membership

Official Recognition

Authorized certification by BTA to operate teleradiology services in Bangladesh.

Collaboration & Networking

Opportunities to collaborate and share expertise among top professionals.

Ethical Guidance

Compliance with ethical standards, ensuring trust and patient safety.

Training & Events

Access to workshops, events and knowledge-sharing programs.

Membership Rules

  • All members must follow ethical guidelines and BTA policies strictly.
  • Non-compliance may result in suspension or cancellation of membership.
  • Membership renewal is mandatory every year within the due date.
